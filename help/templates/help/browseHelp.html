Browse Preview
In the browse part, you can view all EHFPI entries (see next section for direct link). All pathogens that have validated EHF information in EHFPI are displayed as according to taxonomy classification (e.g., kingdom, group, family, genus, and species).
Or you may choose a pathogen of interest and the EHF results are presented as a two-level preview. In the first level preview, a summary of RNAi screens with publication title and phenotype information about all strains/subtypes tested under the same pathogen name are listed. Here is an example result list for viral specie HIV-1. The left column provides information for all tested HIV strains.
Click the link for a "publication title" of interest, the second level preview will be displayed, showing details about the corresponding article, screen and EHF genes information. This page provides you a three-part result list. The first part is "article information", including Title, Authors, Journal, Year, PubmedId and Abstract. The second part is "screen information", including screen scope, type, reagent, target organism, phenotype, bioModel description, confirmatory screen description,primary screen description, calculated hits number, confirmed hits number, primary hits number, and note. The third part is the list of article-dependent EHF genes with custom display.

Filter Results
Alternatively, you can browse all EHFPI entries by clicking the link in browse page or here. We provide a result guidance tree for you to filter the entries.
You can filter all entries in EHFPI within four fields:
  • Pathogen
    • Presented at the kingdom and species level only.
  • Year
    • Year of Publication.
  • Journal
    • Journal of publication.
  • Phenotype
    • The knockdown effect of a specific (group of) gene(s) on pathogenic infection, e.g., increased infection, and inhibited infection.
It may take time for the selected results to load, please be patient!
Please note that the size of filter window is adjustable. The number mark at the end of each guidance item represents the total number of EHFPI accessions under that statistical category. The results displayed in the right window will change according to your selection.
Customize Browse Results
If you want to change the default displayed results according to your own research interest, please click the 'Custom Display Columns' button above the Result Table (right).
Then, choose the columns you want to display. Note: ehfpi acc, gene symbol, pathogen species, publication title and phenotype are default displayed columns.
Change the order of a specific columns by clicking the table title, e.g. EHFPI ACC. The first click will result in ascending order, and second click descending order.
Data Export of Results
You can select all records (including all EHFPI information items) or the interested records of on the current page, then click the 'Download' button to obtain the results.
You can also choose the columns you want to for download. The file format is csv.
Analysis of Results
Conveniently, you can perform analysis for all or selected EHF records by click the button above the search result. More details can be found in analysis part.
